About me

I'm Kunal Kabra. I studied mechanical engineering at IIT Bombay, did research in energy systems, and now work at Bain advising companies on growth and operations. Mostly in complex, capital-intensive environments where the real problem is execution, not ideas.

I care about how things actually get built. Not the deck. The system.

This site is where I think out loud.

What I believe

Enduring companies are built by people who understand both the model and the machine. How value is created, and how it gets executed.

Strategy without engineering is hand-waving.

Engineering without strategy is local optimization.

The edge is in combining both, patiently and over long time horizons.

What I'm working on

Right now I'm going deep on a few things: how AI actually changes the leverage available to one person (I build my own tools and workflows, not just use off-the-shelf products), what automation really means for Indian manufacturing at the unit economics level, and why some Indian companies compound while most don't.

Long term, I want to build something in India's industrial economy. Could be manufacturing, could be deep tech, could be somewhere at the intersection. I don't have the full picture yet, but the direction is clear enough.
